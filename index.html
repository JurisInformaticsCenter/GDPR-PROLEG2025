GDPR-PROLEG Workshop 2025

Translating GDPR into PROLEG using Large Language Models (LLMs)

Hybrid Workshop: On-site & Online

November 4-7, 2025

Overview

The GDPR-PROLEG Workshop explores how the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) can be formalized into PROLEG logic representations with the help of Large Language Models (LLMs). This four-day workshop features collaborative evaluations, discussions, and use-case analyses focused on specific GDPR articles.
